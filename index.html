What we do

Humanitarian Gap Initiative (HGI), is a National Non-Governmental Organization (NNGO), created in September 2019, with its headquarters in Juba and field office in Bor, Jonglei, South Sudan.

Tertiary Education Support

Nursery and Primary Education Support

Scholarships for best Students.

Higher Education Support

University Support

We have enabled students to value education as we take the extra mile of assisting them complete their University education without struggle and assisting them find job opportunities so that they can also be able to give back to the community.

About Us

Our Mission

We bring together communities to identify and solve grassroots Humanitarian and developement needs to achieve social and economic outcomes of hope, justice and prosperity. H.G.I intervenes in regions in crisis(Through emergency projects ) , post-crisis(Through rehabillitation projects). Our vision is to have a world where the poor and the marginalized are empowered and have the means to prosperity and freedom.
